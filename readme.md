#  QuickBite Delivery Services Analysis

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes food delivery operations for **QuickBite Delivery Services** using **Microsoft Excel**. The objective is to evaluate restaurant performance, rider productivity, delivery efficiency, customer behavior, and operational trends through an interactive dashboard. The analysis helps identify key business insights and provides actionable recommendations to improve delivery performance, reduce cancellations, and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

##  Business Problem

Analyze the provided order and delivery data to evaluate restaurant performance, rider productivity, delivery efficiency, customer behaviour, and operational trends. Provide actionable recommendations to improve delivery performance, reduce cancellations, and increase customer satisfaction.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Evaluate restaurant performance based on orders, revenue, and cancellations.
- Measure rider productivity using delivery completion metrics.
- Analyze delivery efficiency through delivery time and delay analysis.
- Understand customer behavior using payment methods, order value, and customer segments.
- Identify operational trends across cities and time slots.
- Develop an interactive Excel dashboard for business decision-making.
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve operational efficiency.

---

##  Dashboard Highlights

The dashboard answers the following business questions:

- Which restaurants receive the highest number of orders?
- Which cities generate the highest revenue?
- Which delivery partners complete the most orders?
- Which time slots experience the highest delivery delays?
- Which restaurants have the highest cancellation rate?
- Which payment methods are most preferred by customers?
- Which customer segments generate the highest order value?
- How do delivery delays affect customer ratings?
- What recommendations can improve delivery efficiency and customer satisfaction?

##  Key Insights

- Coimbatore generated the highest revenue among all cities.
- Dinner time recorded the highest customer spending and order value.
- A few restaurants contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Most customers preferred digital payment methods over cash.
- Successful deliveries accounted for the majority of total orders.
- Customer ratings indicate opportunities to improve service quality.
- Delivery performance varied across different time slots and locations.
- Operational insights support better rider allocation and restaurant performance monitoring.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Increase rider availability during peak dinner hours.
- Improve operational support for restaurants with high cancellation rates.
- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing restaurants.
- Promote digital payment offers to improve customer engagement.
- Introduce loyalty programs to increase repeat customer purchases.
- Optimize delivery routing to reduce delays and improve customer satisfaction.

---

##  Project Outcome

The project demonstrates how Excel can be used to transform raw operational data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, KPI development, interactive dashboards, and analytical storytelling. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators and make informed business decisions.
